Patattie Games is the two-person indie punk duo made up together with the creative doodler Murray Somerwolff.

![The Team](/media/patattie_profile.jpg)

## Wax Heads

Our debut title, **Wax Heads**, is an award-winning indie game that explores unique mechanics and artistic direction.

[![Wax Heads](/media/games_capsule/wax_heads.jpg)](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2769240/Wax_Heads/)


**Features:**

- Gorgeous comic book art style
- 70+ hand drawn records
- Quirky customers with unusual tastes
- An uplifting narrative about community, mystery and underdog spirit!
- An immersive handcrafted musical world
- Charmingly approachable puzzle design
- Mini games… and virtual pets!
- Killer original soundtrack
