I'm passionate about exploring new creative directions and experimental game creation through various formats and technologies.

You can find many of my jam games and experimental projects on my **[itch.io profile](https://rothiotome.itch.io/)**.

## Community Events & Jam Organization

I participate in and help organize community game jam events like the [Numerica Twitch Jam](https://itch.io/jam/numerica-twitch-jam), bringing together developers and players for collaborative game creation experiences.

## Twitch Integrations & Exploration

I've explored extensive Twitch integrations and live streaming as a medium for game creation and interaction, experimenting with how games can engage with live audiences and streaming platforms. I've also worked on several titles designing their Twitch integrations - see [client work](/clients/) for more details.
