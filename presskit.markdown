# Rocío 'Rothio' Tomé (She/Her)
### Game Developer - Programmer

---

## About me
Rocío "Rothio" Tomé is the Creative Technical Director and co-founder of Patattie Games, the studio that debuted with the award-winning Wax Heads. A programmer holding a PhD in Psychology with a decade of engineering experience — ranging from experimental game jams to B2B VR applications for medical training — she is a recognised authority on game engines like Godot and Twitch integrations. A seasoned international speaker and online personality, Rocío frequently shares her expertise on major stages such as A MAZE, GodotCon, and Mobile World Congress.

Rocío's frequent contributions to the Spanish and international games industry include the creation of thousands of hours of educational developer-targeted content creation on Twitch and YouTube, co-founding FemDevs, and creating various free open-source tools for developers. She was recognised for these and other efforts with a nomination for the prestigious GameDevHeroes award in 2026.

Blending technical ability with an academic background in behavioural sciences, she serves as a highly versatile consultant and media speaker on the realities of indie development, engine architecture, accessibility, and the psychological impacts of game production.

Rocío 'Rothio' Tomé is a Spanish game developer. She is best known for creating Wax Heads at Patattie Games, the studio she co-founded, her frequent talks at game development and technology events and her public outreach through Twitch, Youtube and social media.
